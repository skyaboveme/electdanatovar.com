# Daña Tovar for Smithville City Council, Place 5

Daña Tovar is running for Smithville City Council, Place 5. The election is on May 2, 2026, with early voting beginning on April 20, 2026.

## About Daña
Daña is a third-generation Smithvillian who has lived in the community for over 40 years. She is not a career politician; she is a neighbor who has been paying attention. She believes the city council should work openly, honestly, and with respect for every person who lives in Smithville. She is invested in the community through volunteering, supporting local schools, and helping neighbors.

## Core Platform (The Three Pillars)

1. Transparency: "You deserve to know."
Daña advocates for plain-language budget summaries, public project updates, and open-door council communication.

2. Accessibility: "I answer the door."
Daña commits to regular community office hours, responding to every resident inquiry, and attending neighborhood gatherings year-round. You shouldn't have to navigate red tape to get a straight answer.

3. Deep Roots: "Over 40 years and counting."
Daña has been part of Smithville's growth, its challenges, and its wins. She cares deeply about what happens next and believes that to have a strong community, you must help build it.
